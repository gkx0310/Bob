### Machine 1: Resistance ANOVA

We performed a two-way ANOVA to assess the effect of `Pressure` and `Temperature` on `PartResistance` for Machine 1.

- **Pressure:** $Pr(>F) = N/A$ (Significant: N/A)
- **Temperature:** $Pr(>F) = 0.0000$ (Significant: Yes)
- **Pressure*Temperature Interaction:** $Pr(>F) = 0.8692$ (Significant: No)

*(Significance level $\alpha = 0.05$)*
